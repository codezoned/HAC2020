The long lockdowns have left people desiring to go out and socialise. The pandemic has left us with a heightened sense of precaution, one which might very well need to be carried forward. What makes us human is our ability to express ourselves but once our world reopens the precautions still need to be taken, meaning restricted interactions. To build a future where we can transcend these restrictions and continue to interact with our surroundings we need newer ways to interact with them. HAC (Hack Against Covid) is a Hackathon that challenges students to build proactive technology and solutions to safeguard human interaction in the coming future post covid19 pandemic.
